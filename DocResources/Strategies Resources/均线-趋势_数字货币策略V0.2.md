|x|------------------------------------------------------------------------------|分割符号|
|ExitFastPeriod|2|平仓快线周期|
|ExitSlowPeriod|4|平仓慢线周期|

|MAType|0|均线类型: TA.EMA|TA.MA|

                break
            crossNum -= 1
        elif(arr1[i] > arr2[i]):
            if(crossNum < 0):
                break
            crossNum += 1
        else:
            break
    return crossNum
